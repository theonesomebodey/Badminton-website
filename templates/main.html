Tournament Details

Event Overview

Join us for the badminton event of the year! The Dropshots For A Cause promises thrilling matches, friendly competition, and a chance to showcase your skills on the court.

  • Date: Saturday, August 2, 2025
  • Time: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
  • Location: Surrey Badminton Club, Main Area(Court 1-4)

Eligibility & Categories

Open to all community members.

  • Categories: Co-ed Singles, Co-ed Doubles
  • Skill Levels: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced (self-assessed)
  • Entry Fee: $15 per player

Tournament Format

The tournament will feature a round-robin group stage followed by a single-elimination knockout stage for the top performers in each category.

  • Each match will be best of 3 games, rally scoring to 21 points.
  • Minimum of 3 matches guaranteed for all participants in the group stage.
  • Shuttles will be provided.

Prizes & Awards

Exciting prizes await the champions and finalists!

  • Champions: Gold Medal $50 Gift Voucher per Champion for each event

Our Cause

Dropshots For A Cause is more than just a tournament; it's an opportunity to make a tangible difference in our community.
